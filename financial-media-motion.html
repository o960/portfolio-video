Overview

I worked as a video editor and motion graphics designer on multiple long-form finance and crypto podcast series, producing both full episodes and social cutdowns on a tight weekly cadence. The goal was fast, accurate, brand-consistent output while maintaining editorial clarity across complex topics.

  • Client/Org: Blockworks LLC
  • Role: Video Editor & 3D Generalist
  • Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Blender, Descript, Asana, Megaphone, YouTube Studio, Slack

What I did

  • Edited long-form finance and crypto podcast episodes and created social cutdowns, maintaining clarity and pacing.
  • Designed and applied branded motion graphics, lower thirds, captions, and sound design using Premiere Pro and After Effects.
  • Collaborated with producers to interpret episode intent, incorporate feedback, and refine edits through structured review cycles.
  • Improved efficiency via transcript-based workflows and clean project structure for faster revisions.
  • Delivered platform-ready exports for YouTube/social with spec-safe audio, captions, and branding.

Process

  • Pre-Production: Reviewed outlines/recordings, set templates, and aligned on deliverables (full + shorts).
  • Editing & Motion: Cut in Premiere Pro, applied AE packages, captions, and sound design with pacing/clarity focus.
  • Workflow & Review: Used transcripts + review tools to reduce iteration time and speed approvals.
  • Delivery: Exported to spec for platform, ensuring consistency, readability, and compliance.
