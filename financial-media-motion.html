Overview

What I Did

Edited 2–3 long-form podcast episodes per week alongside short-form social cutdowns for multiple Blockworks shows. Applied branded motion graphics, captions, and audio polish while maintaining editorial accuracy. Worked closely with producers to manage revisions, approvals, and delivery across finance and crypto media channels.

Process

Worked from producer outlines and recorded sessions to assemble initial cuts using Adobe Premiere Pro. Accelerated rough cuts through transcript-based editing with Descript, then refined pacing, sound, and branding through structured review cycles tracked in Asana. Applied After Effects motion packages, cleaned dialogue using Adobe audio tools, and coordinated feedback and approvals before final delivery.

Results

Reduced average edit turnaround from approximately two days to one day per episode by introducing transcript-driven workflows and clearer review pipelines. Supported consistent audience reach of 700–2,000 viewers per episode while maintaining professional brand standards across multiple ongoing series.
